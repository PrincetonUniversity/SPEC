Detailed Description

The Fourier harmonics of the interfaces are contained in iRbc(1:mn,0:Mvol) and iZbs(1:mn,0:Mvol), where iRbc(l,j), iZbs(l,j) contains the Fourier harmonics, \(R_j\), \(Z_j\), of the \(l\)-th interface.
