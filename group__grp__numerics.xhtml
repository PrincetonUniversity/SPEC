Assign Fourier mode labels.

  • This routine assigns the Fourier mode labels that converts a double-sum into a single sum; i.e., the \(m_j\) and \(n_j\) are assigned where

    \begin{eqnarray} f(\theta,\zeta) & = & \sum_{n=0}^{N} f_{0,n}\cos(-n \, N_P \, \zeta) + \sum_{m=1}^{M} \sum_{n=-N}^{N} f_{m,n}\cos(m\theta-n \, N_P \, \zeta) \\ & = & \sum_j f_j \cos(m_j\theta-n_j\zeta), \label{eq:condensedFourierrepresentation_numrec} \end{eqnarray}

    where \(N\equiv\,\)Ntor and \(M\equiv\,\)Mpol are given on input, and \(N_P \equiv\,\)Nfp is the field periodicity.

Forward Fourier transform (fftw wrapper)

  • This constructs the "forward" Fourier transform.
  • Given a set of data, \((f_{i},g_{i})\) for \(i = 1, \dots N_\theta N_\zeta\), on a regular two-dimensional angle grid, where \(\theta_j = 2 \pi j / N_\theta\) for \(j = 0, N_\theta-1\), and \(\zeta_k = 2 \pi k / N_\zeta \) for \(k = 0, N_\zeta -1\). The "packing" is governed by \(i = 1 + j + k N_\theta\). The "discrete" resolution is \(N_\theta \equiv\,\)Nt, \(N_\zeta \equiv\,\)Nz and Ntz \(=\) Nt \(\times\) Nz , which are set in preset() .
  • The Fourier harmonics consistent with Eqn. \((\ref{eq:condensedFourierrepresentation_numrec})\) are constructed. The mode identification labels appearing in Eqn. \((\ref{eq:condensedFourierrepresentation_numrec})\) are \(m_j \equiv\,\)im(j) and \(n_j \equiv\,\)in(j) , which are set in readin() via a call to gi00ab() .
