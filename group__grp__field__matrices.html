Detailed Description

  • The energy, \(W \equiv \int \! dv {\; \bf B}\cdot{\bf B}\), and helicity, \(K\equiv \int \! dv \; {\bf A}\cdot{\bf B}\), functionals may be written

    \begin{eqnarray} W & = & \frac{1}{2} \; a_i \; A_{i,j} \; a_j + a_i \; B_{i,j} \; \psi_j + \frac{1}{2} \; \psi_i \; C_{i,j} \; \psi_j \label{eq:energy_globalmatrix_global} \\ K & = & \frac{1}{2} \; a_i \; D_{i,j} \; a_j + a_i \; E_{i,j} \; \psi_j + \frac{1}{2} \; \psi_i \; F_{i,j} \; \psi_j \label{eq:helicitymatrix_global} \end{eqnarray}

    where \({\bf a} \equiv \{ {\color{red} A_{\theta,e,i,l}}, {\color{blue} A_{\zeta, e,i,l}}, {\color{Orange} A_{\theta,o,i,l}}, {\color{Cerulean}A_{\zeta ,o,i,l}}, f_{e,i}, f_{o,i} \}\) contains the independent degrees of freedom and \(\boldsymbol{\psi} \equiv \{\Delta \psi_t,\Delta \psi_p\}\).
  • These are allocated and deallocated in dforce(), assigned in matrix(), and used in mp00ac() and (?) df00aa().
