Detailed Description

  • To facilitate construction of the metric integrals, various trigonometric identities are exploited.
  • The following are used for volume integrals (see volume() ):

    \begin{eqnarray} a_{i,j,k} &=& 4 \; m_k \oint\!\!\!\oint \!d\theta d\zeta \,\,\, \cos(\alpha_i)\cos(\alpha_j)\cos(\alpha_k) /(2\pi)^2 , \\ b_{i,j,k} &=& 4 \; m_j \oint\!\!\!\oint \!d\theta d\zeta \,\,\, \cos(\alpha_i)\sin(\alpha_j)\sin(\alpha_k) /(2\pi)^2 , \end{eqnarray}
